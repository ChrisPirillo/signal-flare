- **TikTok Creator Fund / Creativity Program:** Creator support

- **TikTok Shop sellers:** Seller support
- **Advertisers:** Account managers

## Known organizational gaps

- **Gap 1: In-app appeals only.** Many TikTok appeals must happen through the in-app "appeal" button, which is unavailable if the account is fully disabled. Web-based escalation is limited.
- **Gap 2: Algorithmic enforcement with high false-positive rate.** Documented pattern of legitimate content being removed; account-level enforcement amplifies.
- **Gap 3: Cross-border concerns.** US users dealing with ByteDance parent company face additional complexity for international correspondence.

---

## Documented patterns of failure

- **Mass content removal waves** affecting specific topics (LGBTQ+, political, health-related)
- **Shadow banning** without clear notification or appeal
- **Slow appeal resolution**
