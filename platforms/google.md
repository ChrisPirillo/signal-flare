## Special-status escalation paths

- **YouTube Partner Program (YPP) creators:** Have access to Creator Support; large channels have YouTube Partner Manager
- **Google Workspace customers:** Business support tier with phone/chat
- **Google Cloud customers:** Enterprise support
- **Google One paid subscribers:** Premium support for storage/account issues

---

## Known organizational gaps

- **Gap 1: YouTube vs. Google Account Trust & Safety.** YouTube enforcement actions can disable the underlying Google Account, but the YouTube and Account teams operate separately. Cases stuck between them are common.
- **Gap 2: Automated YouTube channel termination with limited appeals.** YouTube terminates channels via automated systems; appeals route to similar automated review.
- **Gap 3: Google Workspace admin issues.** When a Workspace admin's personal account is affected, the entire organization's Workspace can be impacted with no clear escalation path.

---

## Documented patterns of failure

- **YouTube channel termination for "circumventing"** — pattern of creators terminated for vague "circumventing" violations with limited appeal
- **Mass automated terminations** affecting legitimate creators
- **AdSense/monetization disputes** — billing-adjacent issues that fall between teams

---

## Regulatory precedents

- **FTC YouTube COPPA settlement (2019):** $170 million settlement; ongoing compliance obligations
- **Multistate AG actions:** Multiple, including consumer protection cases
- **EU DSA / DMA:** Google is designated VLOP and Gatekeeper

---

## Asset preservation specifics

- **Google Takeout:** https://takeout.google.com — export from any Google service
- **Channel transfer:** Limited; YouTube channels can be transferred to Brand Accounts but ownership is complex
- **Restoration windows:** Disabled accounts retained ~20 days; YouTube terminated channels often unrecoverable

---

## Public-pressure channels

- **@TeamYouTube on X** — most responsive YouTube support channel, sometimes
- **YouTube Creator Help Community** — community moderators occasionally elevate

---

## Anti-patterns specific to Google

- **Don't create new Google accounts to circumvent.** This violates ToS.
- **Don't appeal repeatedly with the same evidence.** Adds noise without value.
- **Don't pay anyone for "Google account recovery."** Scams.

---

## Platform-specific harm narrative angles

- Loss of Gmail (often a primary email used for many other services)
- Loss of YouTube channel with audience built over years
- Loss of Drive content (work, personal documents)
- Loss of Google Photos (years of family photos)
- Loss of monetized YouTube revenue
- Disruption of Google Workspace business operations
