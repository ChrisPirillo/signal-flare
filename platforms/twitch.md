## Special-status escalation paths

- **Twitch Partner program:** Partner Help via partnerhelp@twitch.tv (Partners only)
- **Twitch Affiliate program:** Limited separate channel
- **Top streamers:** Often have direct Partner Manager contact

---

## Known organizational gaps

- **Gap 1: DMCA strikes vs. account standing.** Music DMCA strikes can disable accounts even when the streamer was authorized; appeal process is poor.
- **Gap 2: Off-platform conduct policy.** Twitch's "off-platform conduct" enforcement is opaque; bans for behavior outside Twitch happen with limited explanation.
- **Gap 3: Mass-DMCA episodes.** Major music industry DMCA waves (2020 onward) have permanently affected many legitimate creators.

---

## Documented patterns of failure

- **DMCA-related account harm** with limited recourse
- **Slow ban appeal resolution**
- **Inconsistent off-platform-conduct enforcement**

## Asset preservation specifics

- **Data export:** Available via Settings → Privacy → Request your data
- **VOD/highlight backups:** Streamers should download proactively
- **Restoration windows:** Disabled accounts retained ~30 days typically

## Anti-patterns specific to Twitch
