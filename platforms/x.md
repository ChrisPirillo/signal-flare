## Special-status escalation paths

- **X Premium subscribers (paid blue check):** Have access to "premium support" but the response quality has been inconsistent post-2023 reorganization
- **X Verified Organizations:** Have account managers
- **Content creators with revenue sharing:** Creator support channel

---

## Known organizational gaps

- **Gap 1: Post-2023 staffing reductions.** X significantly reduced its Trust & Safety and customer support staff in 2022–2023. Standard support response times and quality have been inconsistent since.
- **Gap 2: Algorithmic suspension with limited human review.** Many suspensions are issued by automated systems with appeals routing back to similar automated review.
- **Gap 3: Premium support ≠ recovery support.** Paying for X Premium does not unlock account recovery for suspended/hijacked accounts; it provides priority for routine support only.

---

## Documented patterns of failure

- **Mass automated suspension waves** — multiple documented incidents post-2023 of legitimate accounts being suspended in batches
- **Verified-account hijacking** — high-value targets for attackers
- **Slow appeal resolution** — appeals often take weeks to months

---

## Regulatory precedents

- **FTC consent order:** 2011 FTC order remains in effect, recently updated
- **State AG actions:** Multiple states have pursued X on consumer protection grounds
- **EU DSA:** X is a designated VLOP under DSA; subject to EU enforcement

---

## Asset preservation specifics

- **Data export:** Settings → Your Account → Download an archive of your data
- **Account transfer:** Not supported between users; Tweets cannot be transferred
- **Restoration windows:** Suspended accounts may be retained for ~30 days; deleted accounts ~30 days

---

## Public-pressure channels

- **Official handles:** @X, @XSupport, @Safety
- **Direct executive visibility:** Public X posts from executives are sometimes responsive to high-visibility issues

## Anti-patterns specific to X

- **Don't create alt accounts during a suspension.** This is a separate ToS violation.
- **Don't tag the platform owner in posts about your case.** It rarely helps and often backfires.
- **Don't pay for "X recovery services."** Scams.
