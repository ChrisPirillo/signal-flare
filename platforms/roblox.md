## Known organizational gaps

- **Gap 1: Heavy automated moderation.** Roblox enforces extensively via automated systems given user demographics; high false-positive rate.
- **Gap 2: Children's accounts have parental complexity.** COPPA compliance creates additional steps for under-13 account recovery.
- **Gap 3: Robux/economy disputes route separately from account disputes.**
