## Known organizational gaps

- **Gap 1: Phone-number-centric recovery.** Snapchat recovery is heavily tied to the phone number on file. Users who change phones face complex recovery.
- **Gap 2: Limited identity verification options.** Snap has fewer alternative-verification paths than Meta or Google.
- **Gap 3: Streak loss has no real recovery.** Snapstreaks lost during account issues are essentially unrecoverable, even with successful account recovery.
